I am a second-year PhD student at HKUST supervized by Amir Goharshady. My research is supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme.

My research interests relate to the overall group focus on program verification, where I am particularly interested in the parameterization of SMT solvers. I have also worked in probabilistic cache management, abstract interpretation and Markov decision processes.
